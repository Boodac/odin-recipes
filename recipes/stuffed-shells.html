Stuffed Shells

Description

Jumbo Shells stuffed with Ricotta filling and baked with beef ragu

You Will Need

  • 13x9 Baking Dish

Ingredients

  • Jumbo Shells - 12oz
  • Ground Beef - 16oz
  • Marinara - 16oz
  • Ricotta Cheese - 15oz
  • Parmesan - 4oz
  • 1 Large Egg
  • Mozarella - 8oz
  • Parsley - 1/4 Tsp
  • Garlic Powder - 1 Tsp

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven tp 350° F
  2. Bring salted water to a boil. Following your box's directions, prepare your jumbo shells. After they are drained, ensure you rinse them with cold water to stop the cooking process.
  3. Brown the ground beef, draining any excess fat. Combine it with your marinara (jarred or homemade).
  4. Prepare the shell filling by combining the ricotta, a few ounces of mozarella, the parmesan, egg, parsley and garlic powder. You may add black powder to taste.
  5. Line the bottom of your baking dish with a small amount of the sauce that you've created.
  6. Using a spoon and your fingers, fill each shell (careful not to overfill) and place them, opening side up,
  7. Top the shells with your mozarella, and cover the entire baking dish with aluminum foil.
  8. Bake for 40 minutes. Remove the foil.
  9. Bake for an additional 5-10 mminutes, until the cheese is brown and bubbly.
  10. Allow the shells to rest before eating, so that the cheese filling can set. Serve with basil garnish.
