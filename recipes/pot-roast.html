Pot Roast

Description

A slow cooker pot roast, served with carrots and potatoes.

You Will Need

  • A Slow Cooker, 7qt+ preferable
  • A Cast Iron Pan, at least 10 inches

Ingredients

  • A large de-boned chuck roast, at least 2lbs but 3 would be better
  • A pound of new potatoes
  • A pound of thick baby carrots; petite-cut will not work. You may find thicker baby carrots by purchasing "organic."
  • A yellow onion
  • 24oz Beef Stock
  • Soy Sauce
  • Worchestershire Sauce
  • Herbs and Spices: Thyme, Cumin, Rosemary, Kosher Salt
  • A Bay Leaf

Steps

  1. Pat your roast dry, and liberally apply kosher salt to all sides.
  2. Sear all sides of the chuck roast in your cast iron pan. Just get it ripping hot and it should take about a minute per side.
  3. Roughly chop an onion, throw it in your slow cooker
  4. Add the potatoes on top of the onion
  5. Add the carrots on top of the potatoes
  6. On the vegetables, sprinkle thyme and rosemary, add a bay leaf, and a few shakes of soy sauce and worchestershire
  7. Place the beef roast on top of the carrots. Don't bother trying to mix anything in.
  8. Pour the beef stock over the beef. Stop if it comes halfway up the side of the meat, otherwise add it all. The vegetables and beef will add more liquid.
  9. Cover the beef with a few sprinkles of cumin.
  10. Cook on low for 6-8 hours.
