Tuna Salad

Description

A crunchy tuna-salad, perfect for sandwiches or dips

You Will Need

  • A Can Opener

Ingredients

  • Can of Tuna
  • 1/2 Cup Celery, diced
  • 1/2 Cup Onion, diced
  • 1/2 Tsp Yellow Mustard
  • 1 Tbsp Dill Relish
  • 1.5 Tsp Sweet Relish
  • 1/4 Tsp Dill Weed
  • 1/4 Tsp Prepared Horseradish
  • Dash of Lemon Juice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup Mayonnaise (or similar)

Steps

  1. Drain the can of tuna thoroughly
  2. Add all the ingredients except the Mayonnaise, combine thoroughly
  3. Add Mayonnaise until it just comes together. Leave the salad fairly dry unless it's being served as a dip.
  4. If you're serving it as a dip (great with Fritos Scoops), add the rest of the mayonnaise.
  5. If you're serving it as a sandwich filling, serve the additional mayonnaise on toasted bread instead.
